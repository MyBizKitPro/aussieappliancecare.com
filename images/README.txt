TIPS
----
- .jpg is ideal for photos. .png and .webp also work (if you use a
  different extension, update the name in the PHOTOS list in index.html).
- Keep a consistent look across all photos (similar lighting / colour
  grade, ideally with teal or warm tones) — that's what makes it feel
  premium and cohesive.
- The hero background stays as-is for now; if you want to replace it too,
  save it as hero.jpg here and change the "hero:" line in index.html.
